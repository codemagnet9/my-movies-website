# Getting Started with PortfolioHub

Welcome to PortfolioHub! This guide will help you get started with creating and submitting your portfolio to our platform.

## What is PortfolioHub?

PortfolioHub is a global platform that showcases personal portfolio websites from developers around the world. We help developers gain visibility and connect with opportunities by featuring their work in a centralized, searchable directory.

## Why Submit Your Portfolio?

- **Increased Visibility**: Get discovered by recruiters, clients, and fellow developers
- **Community Recognition**: Earn votes and climb the featured portfolio rankings
- **GitHub Integration**: Automatically sync your GitHub stats and repositories
- **Global Reach**: Connect with developers from around the world

## How to Submit Your Portfolio

### Step 1: Prepare Your Portfolio Website

Before submitting, make sure your portfolio website includes:

- A clear description of who you are and what you do
- Your best projects with live demos and source code
- Contact information or ways to connect with you
- A professional design that represents your skills

### Step 2: Create Your GitHub Profile

PortfolioHub integrates with GitHub to showcase your:

- Repository count and stars
- Most popular projects
- Programming language distribution
- Contribution activity

Make sure your GitHub profile is up-to-date and includes your best work.

### Step 3: Submit Your Portfolio

1. Navigate to the Submit page on PortfolioHub
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Portfolio website URL
   - GitHub username
   - Country/location
   - Brief description
   - Skill tags
3. Preview your GitHub data to ensure it's correct
4. Submit for review

### Step 4: Wait for Approval

Our team reviews all submissions to ensure quality and relevance. Once approved, your portfolio will be live on PortfolioHub!

## Tips for Success

- **Keep it updated**: Regularly update your portfolio with new projects
- **Engage with the community**: Vote on other portfolios and leave comments
- **Share your profile**: Promote your PortfolioHub listing on social media
- **Optimize for search**: Use relevant tags and keywords in your description
